10/30/23 Week 11
We learned about electrical engineering and how it is the study, design and use of appliences
and equiptment using and form of electricity. There are six main branches of electrical engineering
including, Electronic, computer, control, power, telecommunications and signal processing. Voltage
is how much electicity, current or amps is the speed of flow of the electricity, ressistance or
ohms is the slowing down of electricity and the power or watts is the overall output of electicity.
A diode is a one way switch for an electrical current, a common one is an LED. Resistors reduce the
flow of an electical current in a circut.
