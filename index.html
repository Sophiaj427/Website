8/21/23 Week 1
Today's experiment was very exciting. We used pvc pipes that were cut horizontally as a pathway
for a marble. The goal was to get the marble from the starting point to a small bucket about 25
feet away. And everybody in the group had to use a pvc pipe at least once. We all worked together
to make a plan and almost succeeded. Our group made it to the end but unfortunately it bounced
off and did not make it in the bucket. But overall I had a great time!




8/31/23 Week 2
It has been a fun week. We learned all about the different sections of engineering. Mechanical
engineering is the oldest and most extensive of the engineering branches. This week we also
started working in our groups on planning the design of our Rube Goldberg machine. We CADed a 3D
on TinkerCad, and tried to test it out. It's going to take a little more planning. On Thursday we
Made the model of it CADed a home appliance,I made a microwave. Which I think turned
out well.



9/7/23 Week 3
This week has gone by fast. We planned out our Rube goldburg design in tinker cad, and had to
re-make it because our measurements were off. But we did get the measurements in order. We were
able to complete our design in tinkerCad.Thursday we started to build our design. We had thought
it may be too long but the teacher approved. We measured and cut out the walls, and taped two of
them together. Can't wait to complete it next week!




9/14/23 Week 4
Building our Rube Goldburg machine went smoothly. All the measurements turned out to be right.
And we used as little tape as possible. Though we did have to re-enforce our ramps and paths
so they would hold the car and baseball up. It was really fun putting it together after we cut
all of the pieces out. My job was to cut the tape pieces out for the group, and put them in
place. I learned it was easier to cut the tape with the box cutter than the scissors. It worked
perfectly when we tested it!
